SMART SKINCARE

Find products that truly match your skin.

The skincare market is growing rapidly, with an overwhelming number of products available. While more choices can be helpful, they often make it difficult for consumers to know which products are right for their skin. The best way to choose skincare is by understanding product ingredients and how they match your skin type, but this can be challenging for most people.

Our app simplifies this process. After a short skin type survey, we analyze skincare product ingredients and recommend up to 30 products that best match the user’s skin type. Users can then easily explore and purchase them through platforms like Sephora, TikTok Shop, or Amazon.
